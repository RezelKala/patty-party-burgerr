Our Story

Patty Party Burger started in 2019 from a tiny home kitchen in Jakarta. Our founder, Rizky, had one simple goal: to create the kind of burger that made you close your eyes on the first bite.

Within 6 months, word spread fast. The neighbourhood loved it. Friends brought friends. And soon, that tiny kitchen became our first proper burger joint in Alam Sutera.

Today we serve hundreds of happy customers every day — but our recipe stays the same. Fresh, never frozen. Made with care. Always worth it.
